To What Matters

I’ve spent a lot of time reflecting on the mistakes I made and how I hurt you in ways that I can’t just undo. This collection is a way of showing that I understand the weight of my actions and how deeply sorry I am for not treating you with the respect, care, and love that you deserve.
These photos hold memories of everything we shared, and I see now how my choices hurt the very person I loved and cared for most. This collection is a reflection of that regret, a way of acknowledging the pain I caused while holding onto the moments that still mean the world to me.
I know that no amount of words can erase the hurt I've caused, but I hope these moments, captured in these photos, can serve as a reminder of the love we once shared. What we had will always hold a special place in my heart, and I regret not fully appreciating you in the ways you deserved. I can’t change the past, but I can promise to learn from it. To be more present, more respectful, and to treat you and the people I care about with the patience and kindness that they deserve.
I’m not asking for forgiveness, nor do I expect anything in return. I simply wanted to create this space to acknowledge the mistakes I’ve made and express just how deeply I care. The time we spent together was meaningful, and I will forever cherish the lessons, the love, and the memories. I want you to know that I haven’t given up. I've been actively working on this, putting in the time and effort to show you that I’m committed to making things right, and I will continue to do so. Every small gesture, every little act.
Thank you for being a part of my life. I hope that, no matter where we are in the future, we can both move forward with the understanding that we’ve both grown. And, if possible, I hope this collection serves as a small step towards healing for both of us.
